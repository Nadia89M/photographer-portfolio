Since you ask…

Born & raised in Scotland, I completed a B.Sc in Pure Mathematics before relocating to London to study photojournalism and launch my photographic career in 1985

Although I began working for fashion trade magazines and PR companies, I’ve worked extensively for the retail, financial, legal and technology sectors and my work has featured in many newspapers, magazines, in advertising, annual reports & corporate communications publications

Many of my personal projects have been shown in London galleries, notably two exhibitions at Harrods and most recently my longstanding project on redheads

I’m a visiting lecturer at the University for the Arts London where I’ve taught Professional Photographic Practice since 1995. More recently I’ve been a tutor on the Graduate Diploma in Photography course and also teach several courses on photographic technique & visual culture for other colleges

Over the years I’ve held workshops for many clients on effective briefing of photographers, getting press releases published and taking better pictures

I live with my redhead wife Susan and our two cats in the tax haven and tropical paradise of Southeast London
